Fuel economy of your vehicle!

How many miles can your car go with one gallon of fuel in it?

Let's find it!

Learn more about this

How it works? It's simple

Fill the tank of your vehicle to it's limit. Note the fuel amount filled and the reading of odometer in this application. The system will keep it safe for you. Next time when you refill your tank to it's limit, do the same thing again. This time the app will tell you the fuel economy of your car

What you need? Nothing much.

To use this app you only need to register and add your car. You can use any name of your choice to add a car. Once the car is in the system, all the fuel related records could be added to the car.
You can add multiple cars.

What is coming? Features you'll see in future releases

There is a lot which we want to accomplish through this small app. Some of the major features which you should look for in the future releasees are as follows:

  • Recording the gas(petrol) pump used. So that you could choose your favorite pump.
  • Recording the cost of the fuel.Save that hard earned money
  • Comparing the fuel economy of your different vehicles.
